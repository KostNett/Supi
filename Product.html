How Supi Works

Supi is an AI-powered platform that helps users find the supermarket in which they can get their needs in the lowest possible value in total.It's purpose is to save customer's time of reading and searching through the stores about the different prices.On the other hand Supi is doing it for him/her in such a quick span of time.Thus the customer is reassured about his/her daily shopping list and the store that will benefit him the most.
